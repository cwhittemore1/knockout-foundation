Christopher Whittemore

Visual Designer & Interface Engineer

I am a product and branding designer with an added splash of UX engineering skill, living and working in Jacksonville, Florida. My work and work ethic rely heavily on user-centered design principles to produce high-quality visuals; from concept to execution I seek to implement intelligent solutions in new and creative ways.

When not at work, I actively challenge my body as well as my mind. Whether going for long walks with my pooch Bruzer, finding new routes on the walls of my local rock climbing gym, or hiking through wooded state parks, I've found comfort in an energetic and enterprising personal life.
